How to cook Jollof Rice

There are many way. But here is a simple one

Ingredients

Below are the needed ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Beef
  • Salt

Procedure

Below are the procedure

  1. Wash and Boil the rice
  2. Add Salt and beef
  3. Stir Repeatedly
  4. Serve while Hot
